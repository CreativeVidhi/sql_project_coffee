# sql_project_coffee
Objective : Monday Coffee plans to open 3 new physical coffee shops in India.  The company has strong online sales data since Jan 2023.  Goal: Use SQL-based analysis to identify the top 3 cities for expansion.
Monday Coffee Expansion Analysis
Business Objective
•	Expand by opening 3 coffee shops in top Indian cities
•	Use historical online sales data (since Jan 2023)
•	Recommend best cities based on data-driven insights
Key Questions – Easy to Medium
•	Estimated coffee consumers per city (25% of population)
•	Total revenue in last quarter of 2023
•	Units sold per coffee product
•	Average sales per customer per city
Key Questions – Medium
•	Top 3 selling products in each city
•	Unique customers per city
•	Average sales vs average rent per customer
Advanced Analysis
•	Monthly sales growth trend
•	Market potential comparison across cities
•	Sales vs rent vs customer density
Decision Criteria for City Selection
•	High total sales and strong growth
•	High average sales per customer
•	Large estimated coffee-consuming population
•	Rent sustainability compared to revenue
Final Recommendation
•	Rank cities using combined metrics
•	Select top 3 cities with highest business potential
•	Support recommendation with sales, customer, and population data
